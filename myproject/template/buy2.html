PAY ON DELIVERY

Pay on Delivery (POD) includes Cash on Delivery (COD) and additional digital payment facilities at your doorstep.You can pay for Pay on Delivery orders by cash at all locations and by card in selected locations.

To pay using card swipe machine/mPOS:

  1. Ask your delivery agent for a card swipe machine.
  2. Swipe/insert your card.
  3. Enter your card ATM PIN to complete the payment.
  4. After posting the successful payment, you'll get a confirmation message.

To pay using Our Pay link:

  1. Ask your delivery agent for a Pay link SMS on your smartphone that has internet connectivity.
  2. Select the link in the SMS and initiate payment.
  3. Select your payment method and complete payment using your credentials.
  4. After posting the successful payment, you'll get a confirmation message.
